About

Founded in 2022 by friends Syed Huzaifa Ali and Ansh Kumar,Lets Find Missing is a Pakistan charity that provides specialist support to people who are or at risk of missing, and the families and friends left behind. Our free, confidential helpline is available by phone, text and email to support missing children and adults, and their loved ones.Our mission is to be a lifeline to every missing person, and every family member and friend left behind.
